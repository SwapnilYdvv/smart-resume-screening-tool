Data Analyst

We are looking for a Data Analyst to turn business data into useful insights. The candidate should be comfortable using Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, data analysis, data visualization, and communication skills. A bachelor's degree and at least 2 years of relevant experience are preferred.

Key responsibilities:
- Clean and analyse business data.
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders.
- Communicate findings clearly to non-technical teams.
